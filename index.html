Privacy Policy

For Soul: An Incremental Idle Game · Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SomnaraDev ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Soul: An Incremental Idle Game (the "Game"). By downloading or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We have designed the Game to collect as little information as possible. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask you for your name, email address, or any other personal details to play.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We, as the developer, do not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. The Game has no login or account system, no chat or social features, and does not request access to your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or precise location.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The Game saves your progress — such as your currencies, upgrades, achievements, and settings — locally on your own device. This save data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us, and is not accessible to us. If you uninstall the Game or use the in-Game "Reset Game" option, this local data is deleted.

3. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The Game uses a small number of third-party services to show advertisements and to process optional in-app purchases. These services may collect information automatically when you use the Game. We do not control how these third parties use the information they collect; their use is governed by their own privacy policies.

Advertising — Google AdMob

The Game displays advertisements using Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share information such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, general (coarse) location derived from your IP address, device and diagnostic information, and your interactions with ads. This information is used for purposes including serving and personalizing ads, ad measurement and analytics, and fraud prevention.

You can learn more, and control how Google uses this data, here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

You can reset or limit ad personalization at any time in your device settings under Settings → Google → Ads (the exact path may vary by device).

In-App Purchases — Google Play Billing

The Game offers optional in-app purchases (for example, time-skip boosts, a permanent profit multiplier, and an ad-removal option). These purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment-card details or other financial information; that information is handled entirely by Google. We may receive confirmation of a purchase so the Game can deliver the item you bought. Google's handling of your purchase is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the advertising or purchasing services described above, please contact us at the email below and we will work with the relevant service providers to address it.

5. How to Request Deletion of Data

Because we do not hold any personal data about you on our own servers, there is no account for us to delete. You can remove all data the Game stores on your device at any time by either:

  • using the Reset Game option inside the Game's Settings menu, or
  • uninstalling the Game from your device.

To reset the advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads, use your device's Google → Ads settings as described above. If you have any questions about data deletion, contact us at the email below and we will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

Your save data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's own security. Information collected by the third-party services described above is transmitted and protected according to those providers' own security practices, including encryption in transit.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to access, correct, or delete personal data and to opt out of personalized advertising. Because we do not ourselves collect personal data, most of these rights are exercised directly through Google's tools linked above or your device settings. You may also contact us with any request and we will assist where we are able.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be noted in the Game or its store listing. Your continued use of the Game after an update means you accept the revised policy.
